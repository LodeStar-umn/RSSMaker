RSSMaker

Use RSSMaker to aggregate (collect) RSS feeds from a variety of sources. An instructor can combine a news feed, blog feed, and other sources of information into one view.


RSSMaker is based on the FeedEk jQuery RSS/ATOM Feed Plugin v1.1.2 by Engin KIZIL and is made available to instructors through the MIT License (MIT).
